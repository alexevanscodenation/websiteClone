You’ll still be playing it when it’s vintage.

Clean Make, Clean Sound.

We don’t use bone or hide glue in our workshop, so our guitars are vegan, and with no nasty chemicals, solvents, or polyurethane resins used, the Model 1 happens to be one of the cleanest guitars on the market. Of course, if you’re after a dirty sound, it can do that, too

Dripping With Colour.

The Model 1 comes in eight stock colours, from Wafer Pink to Crowbar Red. We also offer custom colours, so you can finally match your axe to your stage outfit. We use a signature water-based ‘thin skin’ finish, which is durable but wears in beautifully, doesn’t react with guitar stands and is considerably less polluting than nitro or PU finishes.

Pickups Pack a Punch.

We’ve fitted the Model 1 with hand-wound Old Guard P90 pickups, made by Bare Knuckle Pickups, who are based in the South West of England, not too far from our own workshop. The P90s create a warm, full tone, with heaps of presence and a sprinkle of grit. Combine these with the lightweight but highly resonant two-piece poplar body, and you’ve got an undeniably punchy electric guitar.

Shoegazers and Noisemakers, Meet Your Common Ground.

We know what it’s like. Your dial is permanently set to “hazy psychedelia”, but your pal is more into industrial. Or maybe you’re a post-punk purist, but your friend is an unabashed jangler.

It’s a gulf that can’t be bridged, a difference that can’t be reconciled. Well, to this we say: no more arguments, no more awkward silences. The Model 1 from Trent Guitars is here to give you something you can agree on.

Handmade on the South Coast of England, it’s a truly premium axe that will take your playing to a new level, whether you trade in face-melters or tearjerkers. As for your friend’s taste in music? We just make the guitars, we can’t tell anyone what to do with them.
